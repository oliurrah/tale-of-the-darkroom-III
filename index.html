About The Event

About The Event

The AIUB Photography Club is hosting a national photography exhibition titled "Tale of the Darkroom III," which is the third exhibition under the same name. The previous two exhibitions were open to participants from colleges and universities across Bangladesh and were of a high standard. This year, the club is expanding the event by inviting schools and young photography enthusiasts from all over the country to participate. The AIUBPC has been organizing exhibitions since 2011 and is committed to promoting the art of photography in Bangladesh.

Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

Bangladesh Shilpakala Academy, Dhaka

Bangladesh Shilpakala Academy is a national institution that promotes and preserves the arts and culture of Bangladesh. It offers programs, exhibitions, and events in various departments, including music, dance, drama, fine arts, and folklore, to celebrate the country's rich and diverse cultural traditions.

F.A.Q

  • What is "Tale of the Darkroom III"?

    "Tale of the Darkroom III" is a national photography exhibition showcasing the best work of photographers from all over Bangladesh.

  • What is the admission fee for the exhibition?

    Admission to the exhibition is free and open to the public. Everyone is welcome to attend the exhibition and experience the beauty of analog photography.

  • Will there be any photography workshops or talks held during the exhibition?

    Yes, there will be several workshops and talks held during the exhibition on various topics related to photography. These events are open to everyone and will be free of charge. Please check the exhibition's website or social media pages for more information.

  • Are visitors allowed to take photographs of the exhibited works?

    Visitors are not allowed to take photographs of the exhibited works, as this can damage the artworks and is prohibited by the exhibitors. However, visitors are welcome to take photographs of the exhibition itself and share their experiences on social media using the hashtag #TaleOfTheDarkroomIII.

  • Is the exhibition accessible for visitors with disabilities?

    Yes, the exhibition is fully accessible for visitors with disabilities. The AIUB Auditorium is equipped with ramps and elevators, and there are accessible restrooms available. Additionally, exhibition staff will be available to assist visitors with any accessibility needs.

  • Is the exhibition suitable for all ages?

    Yes, the exhibition is suitable for all ages. Children and families are welcome to attend and explore the beautiful photographs on display.

  • Number of photos per story: 5 to 12
  • Photo Series must be submitted in a ZIP file renamed to: Participant’s Name_Series Title_Country_Email address_Contact No For example “Oliur rahman_coexistence_Bangladesh_oli@gmail.com_+8801*********”

  • Submit your story through the Google form (Photo Series)
  • Include a doc/docx file describing synopses of your series at the ZIP file. Both Bengali and English synopses are acceptable
